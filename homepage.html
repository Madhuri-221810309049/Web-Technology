Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM), formerly GITAM University. It is a private institute deemed to be university, located in Visakhapatnam,Hyderabad,Banglore. It was founded in 1980 by a group of intellectuals and industrialists of Andhra Pradesh led by M. V. V. S. Murthi.
